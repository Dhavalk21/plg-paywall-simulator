PLG Growth & Monetization Playbook

Strategic Framework

1. The Organic Growth Loop (K-Factor)

Free users drive organic user acquisition by sharing your product. If you restrict the free tier too much, your viral loop constant (K-Factor) collapses, and customer acquisition costs (CAC) rise rapidly.

2. Feature Elasticity Curve

Locking essential core features (like saving files) creates user frustration. Locking advanced team-oriented features (like permission controls) drives high-conversion updates.

3. Support Overhead Spike

Lowering free usage limits triggers support ticket overflows. The simulator projects both customer service overhead expenses and brand reputation impact.
